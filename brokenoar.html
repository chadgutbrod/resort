The Broken Oar


Our former "Whitetail" cabin is now a beautiful brand new two bedroom, two full size bathroom lakeside lodge! This unit has been renamed the "Broken Oar" with special amenities including a spacious loft with a full size futon, snack bar, huge screened in porch, 42 inch plasma TV with cable, WIFI, and a window wall overlooking the lake which is only steps away!

This unit rents for $2,000 per week during the peak season. A 10% discount is available during the off season. Please see 'Information' for more details!
