The Loon Lodge

"The Loon Lodge" is a beautiful three bedroom, two bathroom two story lodge. The Loon Lodge has woods and water views, with numerous amenities including snack bar with bar stools, an awesome stone gas fireplace, dishwasher, upstairs loft, huge deck with patio furniture and grill and much more!! It is beautifully furnished with brand new upnorth decor, adding to the great northwoods atmosphere. You will absolutely love this cedar sided "home" for your vacation choice with us!

This unit rents for $2,000 per week during the peak season. A 10% discount is available during the off season. Please see 'Information' for more details!
